About Lok Namah Group & LNG Sanitary

Lok Namah Group Pvt. Ltd. - Trusted Name for LNG Sanitary & Products

With over 25 years of industry expertise, Lok Namah Group Pvt. Ltd. was founded by visionary entrepreneur Lokesh Gupta. His in-depth understanding of the sanitaryware industry led to the creation of our own trusted brand — LNG Sanitary — offering premium-quality sanitaryware, bathware, faucets, tiles, kitchen sinks, and allied products.

At Lok Namah Group, we are committed to providing durable, stylish, and functional LNG Products designed for modern homes, commercial spaces, and hospitality projects. Our focus on superior design, lasting quality, and complete customer satisfaction has made LNG Sanitary a trusted name in the market.
